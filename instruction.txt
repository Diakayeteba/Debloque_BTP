# Objective

Create a modern professional portfolio website for a building technician and construction project designer based in Mali.

The website should showcase his experience in architectural drawing, building design, and major construction projects such as mosques, universities, agricultural factories, and private buildings.

The goal of the website is to:
- present his professional credibility
- showcase his major projects
- attract new private clients and construction partners
- allow people to contact him for projects

The site should feel professional, trustworthy, and modern.

---

# Context

The website represents a building drawing technician and construction director from Mali who has participated in several major projects including:

- The Great Mosque of Samaya
- Public Universities in Mali
- Agricultural product factories
- Private construction projects

He has strong experience in:
- architectural building drawings
- construction planning
- project supervision
- technical design for large infrastructures

The website must communicate:
- technical expertise
- credibility
- seriousness
- experience in large scale projects

Target audience:
- private clients
- construction companies
- architects
- investors
- institutions
